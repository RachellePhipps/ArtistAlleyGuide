Artist Alley Guide

Artist Alley Guide is a website designed to help artists looking for conventions to sell their artwork in an artist alley. It contains information about over 100 conventions and their details regarding acceptance to sell.

This website allows users to search for a convention by name, date, and number of attendees. Clicking on a convention will take the user to a special page for that convention detailing its location, about the convention itself, how to apply, and more. Logged-in users are able to leave comments and ratings on conventions they have attended as well as favorite conventions for later.

Currently, this feature is not available for users to add new conventions not already located in the database.

Artist Alley

An artist alley is a designated area at conventions for artists to sell their own artwork and merchandise. This can range from digital art, handcrafts, prints, apparel, and much more. Usually many artists have a mixture of original work and fan-art from existing media.

Many conventions have different criteria when it comes to acceptance into an artist alley. The most common are,
  • Juried
  • Random
  • First-come First Serve
  • Mixed
Juried is when a panel of people decide which artist applications make the cut to be in the conventions artist alley. Random is when random applications are chosen to be accepted. First-come First Serve is when a convention approves artists as they apply. Mixed is when some conventions have a mixture of the above when it comes to accepting applications.

Conventions

Conventions are events where fans of a particular topic/interest gather to participate and hold programs. Most are organized by fans on a not-for-profit basis in their city. They host contests, panels, games, artist alley, actor signing, and general fan events. Main conventions are Comic, Anime, Video Game, Horror, etc
